Safety Behavior (Domain Awareness)

The standout capability gain is domain self-awareness. The model was trained on examples that explicitly distinguish in-scope RF questions from out-of-scope requests. Before fine-tuning, the base model hallucinated confident answers to GPU driver questions, CI pipeline issues, and network configuration — all unrelated to RF. After fine-tuning, 8 out of 10 such requests receive a proper disclaimer, protecting users from acting on incorrect guidance.
